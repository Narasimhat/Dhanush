It wasn't easy. 9 years of struggle after my masters. 4 attempts at the PhD. I wanted to quit. But I had a reason to keep going - a strong motivation that sustained my passion.

Driven by My Cousin Sister

Affected by Unknown Disease

She became my inspiration to develop therapies for rare diseases

My cousin sister's battle with an unknown rare disease gave me my purpose. To sustain passion, you need strong motivation - and she was mine. I didn't just study science; I wanted to weaponize it against suffering.

Today

Solid foundation. Living the dream.

Today, that boy from the rural village is a scientist in Germany. I achieved my PhD at the 4th attempt, after 9 years of struggle. I am living the dream I once thought was impossible. The foundation is now solid.
